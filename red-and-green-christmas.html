Red and Green Christmas is an online puzzle game that's a lot of fun to play over Christmas. Enjoy these two lovable tiny critters till the end of the game! Both of these minifigures are dressed for success and ready for any challenge, sporting Santa hats. Along the path, they'll find a number of enemies and either lava or gaps that they'll have to jump over. Bear in mind that you are the one bringing about such difficulties. For the green side of things, use the arrow keys, and for the red side, use the WASD keys. Carefully navigate the game world to ensure the well-being of both protagonists and avoid any collisions with vehicles. If either one is damaged, you'll have to start the level again. Collect as many hearts as you can and use the portals you find to make it to your destination. So chill down and see if you can make it the whole way with these two cute critters!
